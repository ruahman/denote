\label{sec:org2612847}
-for compound inequalites just do each one seperatly 
\(3x - 4 \leq x - 8\) OR \(6x + 1 > 10\)
\begin{align*}
3x - 4 &\leq x - 8\\
3x &\leq x -4\\
2x &\leq -4\\
x &\leq -2
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
6x + 1 &> 10\\
6x &> 9\\
x &> \frac{9}{6}\\
x &> \frac{3}{2}
\end{align*}
